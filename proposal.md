## Proposal 

- I am building a series of games that tests your ability. Ranging from hangman, to scrabble, to a quick round of sudoku, this will have you questioning your choice. The goal will be that the game starts off fairly easy and over time progresses into something more difficult. 
- This is really for anyone who likes playing brain games, especially the NYT ones. Additionally, I don't think we are challenging our brains enough so this simple game will allow one to work out their brains. 
- Doomscrolling and just being on our phones don't provide much stimulation for our brains. While this website will be a series of fun, short games, it's better than scrolling on TikTok. 
- Some of the features would be that the each game will restart everytime you complete and after 24 hours. Additionally, the goal is to complete 5 games so there will be a section that tracks your progress. If one successfully finishes the 5 games, there is an opportunity to continue playing the games. 
- I am not sure if there is a feature in which it gives the game 24 hours to complete and once it hits those 24 hours it automatically resets (similar to the NYT).
